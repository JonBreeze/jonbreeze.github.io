Room-Temperature Cavity Quantum Electrodynamics

Posted on Sat 30 September 2017 in masers

My new paper in npj Quantum Information on cavity quantum electrodynamics using masers is available free (open access) here:

Room-temperature cavity quantum electrodynamics with strongly coupled Dicke states

In this work, we managed to achieve strong-coupling at room-temperature with a photo-excited spin-ensemble. Similar studies have been presented previously with ensembles of paramagnetic ions, but this is the first time an almost pure collective quantum state had been initialised. Of the \(\textsf{10^{15}}\) molecules, over 90% were in prepared in the excited state.
